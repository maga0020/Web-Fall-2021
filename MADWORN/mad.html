ABOUT

A womans closet is a masterpiece and a mess.

The quote machine below shares fashion tips. The advice is a slim understanding of a persons style becoming a form of art and mastery. You'll notice that among these tips, there is no mention of a hoard-like, ridiculously excessive amount of clothing. Essentially the definition of my wardrobe prior to MADWORN.

Fashion Tips

Piece by piece I decided what was a worthy piece - worthy being a statement piece, a perfectly fitting piece, or a basic necessity; I got rid of the rest. My photographer and I spent countless hours creating outfits, posing, and enhancing lighting to depict the utmost desirable clothing. The name was a combination of my name - MADISON - and the reality of the clothing - WORN. The fish is an ode to living in Florida. The fish design was created with friendly and approachable intentions. Once the logo was created and the photos were edited - everything went to DePop. A platform that not only serves the purpose of a hobby, design experience, and profit generator but a sustainable platform. Turning MADWORN into something bigger is something I look forward to. Don't get MAD, just wear WORN!
